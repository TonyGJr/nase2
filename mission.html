Tony Gunn Jr. Our Mission Forward

  • Build

    Here at NASE, we are passionable about building upon the youth and their ideas. We want to aid them in the thought process and action process of making their ideas realistic and reality.


  • Invest

    An aspect of our mission is to invest into the ideas, individuals, business plans, etc. of exceptional youth by supporting them in business education, financially when funds are available, and in confidence.


  • Disrupt

    Last, but not least, perhaps the most important aspect of our mission is to disrupt the statistics against our youth and prove to themselves that these statistics don't make them!
